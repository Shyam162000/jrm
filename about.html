About Us

History of the School

Jogin Ramanna Memorial High School was established to provide quality education and holistic development for the students of Maski and surrounding regions. Over the years, our school has grown into a center of academic excellence and character building.

School Profile

JOGIN RAMANNA HS MASKI was established in 1991 and is managed by Pvt. Aided. Located in the rural area of LINGASUGUR block, RAICHUR district, Karnataka, the school serves Grades 8 to 10. It is a co-educational institution (no pre-primary section) with Kannada as the medium of instruction. The school operates privately, is not a shift-school, and is approachable by all-weather road. The academic session starts in April and includes educational tours.

  • Private school building with 4 classrooms (all in good condition) and 2 additional rooms for non-teaching activities
  • Separate room for Headmaster/Teacher
  • Barbed wire fencing boundary wall; electric connection available
  • Tap water (functional) as the source of drinking water
  • Functional boys' and girls' toilets (1 each)
  • Playground available
  • Library with 1700 books
  • No ramp needed for disabled children to access classrooms
  • 10 functional computers for teaching and learning
  • No computer-aided learning lab
  • Mid-day meal provided and prepared on school premises
